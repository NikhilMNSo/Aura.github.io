Why AURA

Imagine experiencing a sudden medical emergency while away from home, rendered unconscious due to an underlying health condition. In this critical scenario, valuable time could be lost while healthcare professionals scramble to gather your medical history, allergies, and medication information. This delay in diagnosis and treatment can have significant consequences, potentially impacting the effectiveness of interventions and even jeopardizing your life.

How it works

Our app is your ultimate solution for managing your complete medical history, including surgeries, medications, and allergies. It offers doctors a comprehensive summary of your medical background in the critical "golden hour," ensuring swift and accurate treatment without starting from scratch. What's unique about our app is that all of this is seamlessly achieved through the touch of your fingerprint fingerprint and the convenience of an NFC tag. With just a tap, your vital medical information is instantly accessible, empowering healthcare providers to make informed decisions swiftly, potentially saving lives. But that's not all. Our app goes beyond medical history. It takes care of your entire healthcare journey, from scheduling appointments with ease to ordering essential medicines right from your fingertips. Additionally, we handle all aspects of insurance, ensuring you have peace of mind knowing that your coverage is efficiently managed. Experience the future of healthcare management with our app. It's the ultimate companion for your well-being, putting convenience, efficiency, and ultimately, your health, at the forefront.
